Picture Perfect Trip

Where would you go?

If I could take a vacation, I would go to New York during Christmas time. I would specifically stay in Manhattan near the Rockefeller Center. I would spend time in Central Park and enjoy a few activities there. I would also like to visit Dyker Heights as I hear that there are many cool Christmas decorations in this neighborhood. An afternoon in Bryant Park would also be fun due to the seasonal festivities taking place here.

What would you do?

I would spend a night at the Rockefeller Center and take pictures of the big Christmas tree. I would also love to see the Nutcracker on Broadway. Seeing the Rockettes is also a must. I would do ice skating in Central Park one afternoon. I’ve heard that Bryant Park has a lot of holiday stands, so I could do some shopping there as well.

What would you eat?

New York offers a wide variety of food options, and I would spend a lot of time strolling around and stopping at nearby hole-in-the-wall bodegas to grab bagels, coffee, or sandwiches. Of course, I’d have to try the famous New York-style pizza. While roaming Central Park, I would warm up with a cup of hot chocolate. I would also stock up on candy from the various markets I visit!

Highlights of the Vacation

  • Visit Rockefeller Center and see the tree
  • See The Nutcracker on Broadway for the first time!
  • Try New York pizza and bagels
